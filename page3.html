PLASMA-RADIOLYSIS
POWER GENERATOR

Areas of use

1) Generation of heat and electricity:
- coefficient of heat – up to 2.5x
- Electricity coefficient – up to 3x 

2) Plasma electric furnace for melting and metallurgical processing of metals and alloys: with melting temperature over 3000°C. 

3) In automotive industry the use of a plasma-radiolysis generator may become a source of fast battery charging, as well as the main source of energy in the electric transport, electric circuit and directly on heavy vehicles, ships, etc

Plasma Electric Furnace 

Plasma electric furnace is used for heating, smelting and metallurgical processing of metals and alloys, in which the plasma is a heat source, obtained using a plasma-radiolysis electric power generator in combination with a plasma-water reactor.

Due to the peculiarities of the device of a plasma furnace , it can be used for growing single crystals, processing of pure substances, smelting metals and their alloys with a melting point up to 3200°C.

The furnace can have compact dimensions and can be used both in laboratory conditions and in energy-intensive enterprises.

 A plasma furnace consumes 3-4 times less electricity than any similar equipment. 

Automotive Industry

Objective is a creation of a completely new type of motor for electric vehicles, as well as creating a charger for electric new specifications based on plasma-radiolysis power generator.

Based on the research results obtained, a group of our engineers made the following conclusions on its application: 

  • The plasma generator can be used in the electric circuit of an electric vehicle, taking into account the development of a new generation of electric vehicles, for freight and special vehicles with a capacity of 1 MW for work in quarries and other hard-to-reach and difficult working conditions;
  • The efficiency of using of a plasma generator in the electric circuit of an electric vehicle according to theoretical calculations is at least from 120-130% to 250-300%;
  • The efficiency of using of a plasma generator in the process of charging of the battery of an electric vehicle is more than 200%;

Implementation

  1. The use of a plasma generator in the electrical circuit of pulse charging stations for fast and efficient charging of all types of batteries
  2. The use of a plasma generator in the electric circuit of electric vehicles, as a unit for saving and generating electricity during the operation of electric vehicle
  3. Development and implementation of a new generation electric motor based on a plasma generator with a working efficiency of 120-130% against similar developments of competitors
  4. Finalization and implementation of a compact electric motor 2.0 based on a plasma generator with two electrical circuits and the possibility of autonomous operation for 90 days with the expected characteristics:  mileage at a speed of 60 km/h from 800 to 1000 km on a single charge; plasma generator power - 10 kW; engine power (standard Nissan leaf) - 109 hp (80kw); battery capacity - 24 kW/hour x 2; battery full charge time 220 V - 4.5 hours; time for a full charge of the battery 380 V - 1.5 hours; maximum mileage 145 km/h.
